about

TeaSet is an animation studio based in Vilnius city, Lithuania. We are a team of experienced artists, who create 3D Animation, Design for Commercials, Films and TV. Looking at each project as a chance to grow great ideas, we create individual and creative content from concept through completion. We are proud of friendly relationships with our clients and are always open to new collaboration.
